About Me

Benjamin Ward

GMU Class of 2016

I am currently a student at George Mason University majoring in Applied Computer Science with a focus in Software Engineering. My interest in programing came to me at a very early age, I'd say about sixth grade. I began by making video games and animations using macros in Microsoft Powerpoint way back in the day, and then used Flash to create various applications, mostly for entertainment. I eventually taught myself how to write programs in Java and another tool-based language called GML to write games and other fun applications. Since I have begun college, I have gained a large interest in web development, mobile development (specifically android), and cyber security. I have worked on many personal projects in my free time from schoolwork, including Android applications (can be found on Ben Google's Play Dev), javascript web applications, and web development in general. I work very well in teams and I produce great software when I a working on things I am passionate about. All of my projects can be found in "My Projects" in the top right navigation bar, and I highly encourage you to view my work. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me.
